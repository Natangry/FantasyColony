# Numbers — Capture & Custody (v0.1 Defaults)

## KO & Surrender
- KO threshold: HP ≤ **25%** and Non-Lethal ≥ Lethal → **status.KO** (timer **45–90s**).  
- Surrender base: outnumbered 2:1 **+20%**, 3:1 **+35%**, 4:1 **+50%**; at ≤20% HP **+25%**; fear aura **+10%**; leader nearby **+10%**.  
- Zealous/Undead: **−50%** surrender (or immune if flagged).

## Restraints (escape odds/day baseline)
- Rope: **5%**; Manacles: **2.5%**; Shackles: **1.25%**; Shackles+Gag: **1.0%**; Sedatives: **−50%** for 8h.  
- Watcher: **−4%** (diminishing), Wards: **×0.85** mult, Humane policy **−10%**, Harsh **+10%**.

## Transport
- Carry: **−5%** party speed per captive; Tether: **−2%**; Cage wagon: **0%** speed (consumes capacity, +ambush weight +10%).  
- Camp escape check windows: dusk, midnight, pre-dawn.

## Bounties (coin)
- Common: **50–120**; Notorious: **200–400**; Apex: **800+**; Alive-only **×1.5**.  
- Rep delta: issuer + (3–10), rivals − (2–6).

## Parole & Recruitment
- Parole success: base **60%** → +Humane **+10** / +Chaplain present **+10** / −Harsh **−15**; recidivism base **25%** over 10d.  
- Recruitment gate: min relationship **+20** or shared saga history; Ethos clash reduces chance **−20**.
